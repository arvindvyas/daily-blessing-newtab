# Privacy Policy — Daily Blessing

_Last updated: 1 July 2026_

**Daily Blessing does not collect, store, transmit, or sell any personal or user data.**

## What the extension does
Daily Blessing replaces your new tab page with a daily Bible verse or Bhagavad Gita shloka and a personal prayer/intention list. Everything runs **locally on your device**.

## Data collection
- We do **not** collect any personal information.
- We do **not** use analytics, telemetry, trackers, or advertising.
- We make **no network requests**. All verses and shlokas are bundled in the extension and work fully offline.
- There is **no account, login, or backend service**.

## Data storage
The only data stored is kept **locally** in your browser via Chrome's `storage` API and never leaves your device:
- Your name (used only for the greeting).
- Your chosen tradition, theme, and Sanskrit/prayer-list preferences.
- Your favorited verses and prayer-list entries.

You can remove this data at any time by clearing the extension's storage or uninstalling it.

## Permissions
- **storage** — to save the preferences and favorites listed above, locally.

## Remote code
The extension does **not** load or execute any remote code. All code and content ship inside the published package.

## Content & attribution
- Christian verses: King James Version (public domain).
- Hindu shlokas: Bhagavad Gita, Sanskrit (public domain); Hindi renderings and reflections are original.
