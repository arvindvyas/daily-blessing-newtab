# Daily Blessing — Chrome Web Store listing copy

## Name
Daily Blessing — Verse & Shloka New Tab

## Summary (132 char max)
Calm new tab with a daily Bible verse or Gita shloka and a prayer list. Christian, Hindu, or mix. Hindi & English. Ad-free, offline.

## Description
**Daily Blessing** turns every new tab into a calm, uplifting moment — a daily Bible verse or Bhagavad Gita shloka, plus a simple prayer/intention list.

🕊️ Choose your tradition — **Christian** (King James Version), **Hindu** (Gita shlokas & Hindi reflections), or a **mix** of both.

**Features**
• A fresh verse or shloka every day — tap "Another" for more
• Christian: King James Version (public domain)
• Hindu: Bhagavad Gita shlokas (Sanskrit, public domain) + original Hindi reflections
• Optional Sanskrit shlokas with Hindi meaning
• Personal prayer / intention list
• Favorite, copy, and revisit verses you love
• Light & dark themes, personalized greeting
• Hindi & English

**Private by design**
• 100% offline — no accounts, no servers
• No ads, no tracking, no analytics
• Everything stays on your device

A gentle, ad-free way to start each browsing session with a little peace.

## Category
Lifestyle

## Single purpose
Daily Blessing has a single purpose: to replace the new tab page with a daily inspirational verse or shloka and a personal prayer list, stored locally.

## Permission justification
- **storage**: saves your preferences (name, chosen tradition, theme, favorites, prayer list) locally in the browser. No data is transmitted anywhere.
